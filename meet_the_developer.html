Meet the Developer

"When life gives you lemons, runLemonade"

Hey, I'm Toby and I started with one simple mission in mind... To lower the entry barrier into digital fabrication for makers. So you can spend more time making, building, and spending time with loved ones. Every tool I build comes from real world experience. I've walked the same path you're on, faced the same challenges, and created solutions that actually work in practice. runLemonade is entirely self funded. It's just me, my MacBook, and my knowledge after years of running my own cabinetry shop. I shut the doors in Jan 2025 and the applications and extensions that I am building now are my way of giving back to the making industry.

If you like the apps and extensions that I've built then please share them with your friends, colleagues, and other businesses.
